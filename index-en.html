What is Friendly Medical Care?

A care model centered on respect, confidentiality and accessibility — integrating clinical, mental health and community support for all people.

Why a Carnival?

We connect medicine, education and community through a fun, participatory day that turns learning into action.

Our Goals

Reduce stigma, build consensus on friendly care, mobilize cross‑sector partners, and spark public involvement.

Global Season of Premieres

About the Film — Eyes on Ukraine

Directed by Mo Stoebe. The film follows 24‑year‑old Ukrainian HIV activist Yana Panfilova, exiled to Berlin by the war. Trained by photographer Gideon Mendel to use the camera as her own voice, Yana inspires Liza, Mariia and Yehor to document a unique crisis back in Ukraine.

DCP, Color37 minExec. Producer Richard Gere

Producers: David Gere, Katja Kulenkampff. With: Yana Panfilova, Liza Shevchuck, Yehor Pasko, Mariia Deriuha.

Festivals: Awareness Festival (Oct 2025), Newport Beach Film Festival (Oct 2025), Voices Rising Festival (Nov 2025). Honored at Love Wins Film Festival (May 2025).

World Premiere: Dec 5, 2025 at the Hammer Museum at UCLA (free admission; no reservations).

For more details: https://artglobalhealth.org/event/eyes-on-ukraine-premiere/
