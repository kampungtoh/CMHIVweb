Register About the Film

What is Friendly Medical Care?

Care that centers respect, privacy and accessibility, offering every person friendly, stigma-free, integrated services regardless of age, gender, identity or diagnosis.

Our Goals

Reduce stigma, build consensus on friendly care, rally cross-sector partners, and spark concrete public participation.

What is Friendly Medical Day?

It links healthcare, education and community into an engaging, accessible action day that deepens understanding and support.

Global Premiere Series

This Taiwan screening joins the official “Season of Premieres” for Eyes on Ukraine, following Kyiv and Los Angeles in celebrating resilience, health and human rights.

What is Friendly Medical Day?

Built on cross-sector collaboration, Friendly Medical Day invites medical teams, educators and community partners to co-create a fun, understandable and participatory experience that broadens support for inclusive care.

About the Film — Eyes on Ukraine

Directed by Mo Stoebe. The film follows 24‑year‑old Ukrainian HIV activist Yana Panfilova, exiled to Berlin by the war. Trained by photographer Gideon Mendel to use the camera as her own voice, Yana inspires Liza, Mariia and Yehor to document a unique crisis back in Ukraine.

DCP, Color37 minExec. Producer Richard Gere

Producers: David Gere, Katja Kulenkampff. With: Yana Panfilova, Liza Shevchuck, Yehor Pasko, Mariia Deriuha.

Festivals: Awareness Festival (Oct 2025), Newport Beach Film Festival (Oct 2025), Voices Rising Festival (Nov 2025). Honored at Love Wins Film Festival (May 2025).

World Premiere: Dec 5, 2025 at the Hammer Museum at UCLA (free admission; no reservations).
